## 5 Optimization Tips

1.  **Bet One Universe per Game**\
    Only play favorite or underdog scenarios when your model shows \>60%
    confidence.

2.  **Correlate Props**\
    Stack props that move together (QB yards + WR catches + Opp QB
    attempts).

3.  **EV Filtering**\
    Use model-implied probabilities vs sportsbook odds to reject -EV
    parlays.

4.  **Tiered Bankroll Management**

    -   High confidence: \$15\
    -   Standard: \$10\
    -   Speculative: \$5

5.  **Diversify Across Sports**\
    Split exposure across NFL/NBA/NHL/MLB to smooth variance.
